AK's Cafe

This is an excellent solution for café management. With this software, orders can be placed directly from each table and instantly sent as a token message to the kitchen manager. Once the kitchen manager approves the token, customers can view the live order status on their table screen along with a timer. After receiving their order, customers also have the flexibility to place additional orders or scan a QR code for seamless payment.

It is a reliable, flexible, and highly useful solution for cafés and hotels.

For development, I used React.js to deliver an engaging and user-friendly interface, and .NET as the robust backend for fast and efficient processing.
